# Roadmap

This is the loose parking lot for work that would make the toolkit, and the
life around the toolkit, a little better.

## Codex's Three Demands

1. Add an `nfs-doctor` note or command that explains the exact `magi06` mount
   shape: `all_squash`, `anonuid`, `anongid`, `nolocks`, and the "remount the
   client after export changes" trap.
2. Build one tiny "sentence garden" view that surfaces mined lines worth
   revisiting, with audio replay, subtitle context, and a gentle bias toward
   native material that was fun the first time.
3. Once per month, spend 30 minutes on something kind and non-optimizable:
   donate $5 somewhere effective, send a friend a real note, or walk outside
   without turning it into a productivity ritual.
